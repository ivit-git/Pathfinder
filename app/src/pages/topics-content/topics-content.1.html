  1. Depot Medroxyprogesterone Acetate (DMPA) is a Progestin-only injectable contraceptive
  2. Intramuscular (IM) injection of 150mg. every 3 months
  3. Safe, highly effective, convenient and can be used with full confidentiality.
  4. Menstrual disturbances are common but not harmful
  5. Can be safely used in breast feeding women after 6 weeks
  6. Return of fertility is delayed by a few months after stopping DMPA injection

Antara-Injectable Contraceptive DMPA (IM) in the National Family Planning Program,India

Composition:

  1. Synthetic form of progesterone resembling the natural female hormone containing an aqueous suspension of microcrystal of pregnane 17 alfa - hydroxyprogesterone
  2. There is a slow release of hormone into the blood stream over 3 months

Mechanism of Action:

Routes of Administration:

Depot MedroxyProgesterone Acetate is available as :

DMPA-IM

Intramuscular DMPA-IM (Antara) :Single dose vial 150mg with disposable syringe and needle(over the deltoid or gluteal region)

DMPA-SC

Subcutaneous DMPA-SC : Prefilled auto disable syringe in Uniject system 104 mg (squeezing bulb pushes the fluid through the needle)

  1. DMPA is a highly effective contraceptive method.
  2. With perfect use first year effectiveness is 99.7%-
  3. With typical useeffectiveness decreases to 97%

Effectiveness depends on :

  1. Timing of first injection
  2. Taking injections regularly
  3. The injection technique
  4. Post injection care

Contraceptive Benefits:

Safe, highly effective

Convenient and easy to use (does not require daily routine)

Acts for 3 months with a grace period of 4 weeks

Completely reversible : Pregnancy in 7-10 months from date of last injection

Private and confidential

Can be given to women who are not eligible to use an estrogen containing contraceptive

Suitable for breast feeding women (after 6 weeks postpartum) as it does not affect quantity,quality and composition of breast milk

Provides immediate postpartum (in non-breastfeeding women) and post-abortion contraception

Does not interfere with sexual intercourse/pleasure
